List of New Year movies from Hollywood

  • Title : After the Thin Man

    • After the Thin Man is a movie based on a couple who return home around New Year only to find that they have a family member who has gone missing. They begin to solve the mystery and the story slowly unravels leaving the audience in a good amount of suspense.The film touches upon the genre of comedy drama and can be a good watch for all the murder-comedy fans out there!

  • Release Date : 26th December 1936
  • Genre : Comedy, Drama
  • Title : The Apartment

    • The apartment is a movie about how a man tries to use his own apartment space to help him get promoted but instead finds himself in a very complicated situation and a love life.

  • Release Date : 29th June 1960
  • Genre : Comedy, Romance
  • Title : The Gold Rush

    • The Gold Rush is a great movie starring Charles Chaplin. He tries to move elsewhere in search of gold but ends up meeting a girl and falls in love. And oh! Whats a Chales CHaplin movie if he did not get into some trouble on the way? You'll definetly find some of that as well.

  • Release Date : 26th June 1925
  • Genre : Comedy, Romance
  • Title : When Harry Met Sally

    • When Harry Met Sally is a movie that revolve around two friends who meet after ages. They believe that they can remain friends forever but fate doesnt seem to think the same.

  • Release Date : 12th June 1989
  • Genre : Romance
  • Title : Trading Places

    • Trading places is a movie about the lives of two very different men are switched by two millionares who have placed a bet. The movie revolves around how these two men get accustomed to this new life they have been thrown into.

  • Release Date : 8th June 1983
