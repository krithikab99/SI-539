List of Republic day movies from India

  • Title : URI: The Surgical Strike

    • This is a story of revenge at the border where a surgical strike is plotted to destroy the men who had earlier killed the Indian soldiers.

  • Release Date : 11th January 2019
  • Genre : War, Action
  • Title : Chak De India!

    • Chak de India revolves around a man who was once a great hockey player but couldnt win against an important opponent.Using his grief as a motivation, he goes on to train the girls hockey team to compete internationally.

  • Release Date : 10th August 2007
  • Genre : Sport, Drama
  • Title : Lagaan

    • Lagaan is a story about how a set of farmers in India attempt to resolve a tax issue with the British rulers by playing a game of cricket.

  • Release Date : 15 June 2001
  • Genre : Drama, Musical
  • Title : Shershaah

    • Shershaah is a real life story that shows the life of Captain Vikram Batra and his actions and efforts for the country during the Kargil War.

  • Release Date : 12th August 2021
  • Genre : Action
  • Title : Rang De Basanthi

    • Rang De Basanthi revolves around a set of college friends who act in a political film after the death of their beloved friend who lost his life due to a political mishap.

  • Release Date : 20th January 2006
  • Genre : Romance,Drama
