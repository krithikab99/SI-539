List of Independence Day movies from Hollywood

Mr. Smith Goes to Washington
Release Date 19th October 1939
Genre Comedy, Drama

Mr. Smith goes to Washington is movie about young man with enormous dreams in the political realm. He is asked to go to Washington to become a senate leaves with enormous vision and a good will power but faces many obstacles along the way of achieving his dreams.

Sergeant York
Release Date 02nd July 1941
Genre War, Drama

A small town farmer is suddenly asked to participate in the war. Being someone who strongly believes that war is in no way justifiable, the Sergeant York is a brilliant movie about how he needs to become prepared for war and comes out successful.

Yankee Doodle Dandy
Release Date 6th June 1942
Genre Musical

Yankee Doodle Dandy is a film about George M. Cohan. He has made an enormous name for himself in the musical industry and is also known as the man who owns broadway.

The Longest Day
Release Date 4th October 1962
Genre War, Action

The longest day mentions the events that happened on June 6th (also known as the D-Day).It shows us the perspecgtives and the thinking process of the Germans as well as the members of the Allied.

Patton
Release Date 2nd April 1970
Genre War, Drama

Patton is based on George S. Patton who was an American general. It takes us through his service during the World War 2.
