Mr. Smith Goes to Washington

Release Date 19th October 1939
Genre Comedy, Drama

Its about young man with enormous dreams in the political realm. He is asked to go to Washington to become a senate.He faces many obstacles along the way of achieving his dreams.

Sergeant York

Release Date 02nd July 1941
Genre War, Drama

A small town farmer is suddenly asked to participate in the war. Being someone who strongly believes that war is in no way justifiable, the movie about how he prepares for war.

Yankee Doodle Dandy

Release Date 6th June 1942
Genre Musical

Yankee Doodle Dandy is a film about George M. Cohan. He has made an enormous name for himself in the musical industry and is also known as the man who owns broadway.

The Longest Day

Release Date 4th October 1962
Genre War, Action

The longest day mentions the events that happened on June 6th (also known as the D-Day).It shows us the perspectives and the thinking process of the Germans as well as the members of the Allied.

Patton

Release Date 2nd April 1970
Genre War, Drama

Patton is based on George S. Patton who was an American general. It takes us through his service during the World War 2.The movie takes us through many emotions.
